FAQ

What is Sonr?

Sonr is an ecosystem of decentralized peer-to-peer applications built on top of an identity-based blockchain, which leverages the Cosmos infrastructure. The ecosystem includes tools to empower developers to build decentralized applications.

How can I get involved?

If you’re a developer, check out sonr.dev for information on our available tools, grant programs, hackathons, and bounty opportunity. You can also join our Discord and follow us on Twitter.

Why should I care?

Our interactions and experiences in the newly evolving internet paradigm will be governed by decentralized mechanisms. Sonr equips users for this by putting identity at the core of the ecosystem.

What is in the team’s upcoming roadmap?

We aim to have our mainnet launched and TGE by July. In parallel the team will build out additional developer tools and work with external developers to build decentralized applications in the ecosystem.
